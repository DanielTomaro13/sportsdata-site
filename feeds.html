Manage your feeds

Choose which feeds your licence serves. Stripe tracks how many slots you have; this is where you pick which ones fill them. Saving takes effect on your next restart — no re-download.

⚠ Some gambling & odds feeds are geo-restricted and may not work from your location. There are no refunds for a gambling feed that is geo-restricted where you are.
