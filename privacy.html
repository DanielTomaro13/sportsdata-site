Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This policy explains how sportsdata handles personal information for the sportsdata Service.

1. What we collect

  • Account & billing — your email and subscription details. Card payments are processed by Stripe; we do not store your full card details.
  • Licence & entitlement — your licence key and which feeds it grants, so the Service can verify access.
  • Minimal operational data — e.g. licence-verification requests. We do not receive the contents of your AI conversations — the MCP runs on your machine.

2. How we use it

To provide and secure the Service, process billing, verify entitlements, provide support, and send service/transactional emails. We do not sell your personal information.

3. Who we share it with

  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Resend — transactional email (licence + setup).
  • Cloudflare — entitlement service hosting.
  • Authorities where required by law.

4. Retention & security

We keep account/billing records while your subscription is active and as required for legal/accounting purposes, then delete or anonymise them. We use reasonable technical measures to protect data; no system is perfectly secure.

5. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to legal limits. Contact danieltomaro@icloud.com. This policy is intended to be consistent with the Australian Privacy Principles.
